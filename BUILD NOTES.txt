BUILD NOTES

GAME OVERVIEW

The current stage is the skeleton of the game with most functionalities working. The only thing missing is the assets and proper UI. New songs are easily added as they get directly converted from beat saber maps to maps appropriate to this game.

CURRENT FEATURES:

The features currently available to the player are the following: the ability to use the keyboard to hit notes, with visual feedback when pressing the button associated with a rail. If the note is missed, then it will disappear when hit outside the zone. If a note is hit within the hit zone, then the notes gets destroyed on impact. The notes are decided based on maps available to the player. Currently there is only one map available to be played.
The score is decided through precision. The player starts at 100%, and any note missed will bring the score down.
The game ends when the song is over or if the player hits under 60% accuracy.

CONTROLS:

D for the first rail.
F for the second rail.
J for the third rail.
K for the fourth rail.
Q to quit the game.
